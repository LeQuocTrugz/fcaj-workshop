### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment** 
The working environment is very friendly and open. Members of FCAJ are always ready to support me when I face difficulties, even outside of working hours. The workspace is neat and comfortable, which helps me focus better.

**2. Support from Mentors / Team Admins** 
The organizing committee always provides the best conditions regarding documentation and practical resources (AWS lab accounts) for interns to operate smoothly. Especially, I highly appreciate the guiding methods of the Mentors. Instead of just giving the solutions, they always ask counter-questions and allow interns the freedom to experiment, make mistakes, and self-debug on the system to find the root cause of the problem.

**3. Alignment between Work and Major** 
The tasks in the Bootcamp perfectly complement the Information Technology foundation I learned at school. Not only does it consolidate logical thinking and system architecture, but the program also opens up entirely new aspects that are rarely practiced deeply at school, such as cloud computing. Thanks to this, I can both reinforce my foundational knowledge and learn practical skills.

**4. Opportunities for Learning & Skill Development** 
This internship is a huge leap in both hard and soft skills. Besides mastering AWS services, I also learned how to work effectively in a team, use progress management tools, and especially professional presentation and communication skills that meet the standards of a multinational tech corporation. Mentors also shared many practical experiences to help me better orient my career.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit** 
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, working seriously but still having fun.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns** 
The company provides an internship allowance and flexible working hours when necessary. Additionally, being able to participate in meetups is a huge plus.

### Other Questions
- What are you **most satisfied** with during your internship? 
> It is the opportunity to directly "touch" the core technologies leading the trends (Cloud, AI) and to interact and learn professional working mindsets from top engineers and solution experts in the industry.
- What do you think the company **needs to improve** for future interns? 
> For some labs with complex architectures, the tutorial videos are not really effective. If the program could remake these tutorial videos to be more detailed and of higher quality, interns would have the opportunity to delve deeper into the architecture.
- If introducing it to friends, would you **recommend they intern here**? Why? 
> Definitely **YES**. This is a perfect launchpad and a quality "guarantee" for any IT student who wants to step out of their comfort zone, grasp cloud technology trends, and map out a clear advancement path for their career.

### Proposals & Expectations
*   **Proposals:** I hope that in upcoming Bootcamps, the program can organize more Q&A sessions to resolve difficulties during self-study or provide direct orientation to help interns feel more confident during their internship.
*   **Future Orientation:** The precious experiences at FCAJ are a solid preparation for my upcoming graduation internship. I strongly desire to continue honing my skills and have the opportunity to accompany the AWS ecosystem in my software development career (Backend/Cloud Engineer) in the future.
